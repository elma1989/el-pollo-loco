El Pollo Loco

Welcome to Mexico.

The restaurant-owner Pepe has to catch some chickens for main meal in his restaurant. In Mexico is not a chicken farm available, thats means the chickens are in free wild.

Pepe walks by using LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys and he jumps on hit SPACE.

He can collect some coins or bottles. There are 5 bottles.

The Chickens are dieing, if they are hit by a bottle or if Pepe jumps over there. Pepe thows a bottle, if he hit LEFT CTRL

If Pepe has injured, he can not walk for 3 seconds because of his injury.

BE CAREFUL: The chickens want to revenge by they breaks your actions to their king. The king is very huge to that, Pepe can not jump above him. He only can kill him by hit of thown bottle.

There are 3 hits nesseary, to kill the king. For this reason save your bottles.
